initiative For The Needy

  • Mission
  • Vision
  • Core Values
To improve the quality of life of the local vulnerable communities through substantial local development initiatives.
To fully empower the community in order to upgrade the livelihood of the vulnerable families and individuals in our society.
• Integrity
• Honesty
• Caring
• accountability
Target group
The primary target groups are orphans, vulnerable children and street kids, people living with HIV/AIDS, persons with disabilities, widows, youth, women and older persons who live in Thika, Juja and Ruiru Sub-counties with its township.
The target groups are important and essential component of this project, it is expected that significant changes, learning and development will take place for our target groups and have access to basic needs- education, healthcare, protection and a sense of belonging to the society.
Goal
Our goal is to support the local children, youth, women, orphans and persons with disability through capacity building activities to achieve knowledge skills, network and motivation that will make them aim to have an improved, acceptable living standards, gain human dignity and self-esteem.
